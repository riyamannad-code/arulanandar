What is Generative AI?

Generative AI (GenAI) refers to artificial intelligence systems that can create new content such as text, images, audio, or video by learning patterns from existing data. In higher education, GenAI supports teaching, research, and administration.

Advantages

  • Enhances creativity and productivity in teaching materials
  • Provides personalized learning experiences
  • Automates repetitive tasks like grading
  • Supports multilingual content creation

Limitations

  • Risk of bias and misinformation
  • Ethical concerns around plagiarism
  • Requires digital literacy
  • Dependence on reliable infrastructure
